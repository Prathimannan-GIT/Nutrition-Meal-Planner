Process FAQs

How long does it take to see results?

Most users report visible energy improvements within 7 days and weight changes within 14-21 days of following their plan.

Can I swap recipes manually?

Yes! Our 'Smart Swap' feature allows you to change a meal while the AI instantly recalculates your daily totals to keep you on track.

Do I need to buy supplements?

Our plans prioritize whole-food nutrition. Supplements are only recommended if our AI detects a recurring micronutrient gap in your logs.
